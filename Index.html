Frequently Asked Questions

How does the service work?

Our service provides internet connectivity through satellite to mobile technology. After purchasing a package, you'll receive the internet directly to your phone Simply connect by turning on location and data to access high-speed internet.

Which areas does the service cover?

Our service currently covers urban and suburban(rural) areas across Kenya. We're continuously expanding our coverage. For specific coverage in your area, please contact our support team with your location details.

Can I use Safaricom to purchase bundles?

Yes! We support payments through Safaricom M-Pesa and Airtel. Simply follow the payment instructions provided when you select a package. The process is quick and secure.

What happens after my bundle expires?

Once your bundle expires, your internet access will be suspended until you purchase a new package. You'll receive a notification before your bundle expires, giving you time to renew.
